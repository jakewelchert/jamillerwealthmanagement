We're J A Miller Wealth Management

J.A. Miller Wealth Management is a father/son practice with over 40 years of experience that focuses on working with families by developing a plan for every chapter of life.

  • James A. Miller, CFP®

    Partner - Business Development

    Jim graduated from Creighton University in 1970. He taught English in the Millard School District for 9 ½ years earning a Master’s degree from the University of Nebraska-Omaha in 1974. In 1986, he became a financial professional and earned his Certified Financial Planning Certificate in 1990. He has three children, Josh, Joel, Lauren, and four grandchildren.

  • Joel A. Miller, CFP®

    Partner - Business Development

    Joel graduated from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln in 2006 with a degree in Finance. During his senior year at college, his career as a financial professional started at Northwestern Mutual. In June of 2007 he joined Jim’s practice. He earned his Certified Financial Planning Certificate in 2015. Joel currently resides in Elkhorn with his wife, Tiffany, and their three boys, Jayden, Brooks, and Bennett.

  • Rosemary Napierala

    Office Manager

    Rosemary has been an invaluable member of our team since 2001. Not only could we not navigate the day to day operations without her, she is a great friend to us and many of our clients.

We care about our clients and community.

Although we started our careers in different eras, having great clients has remained a constant. Every day we get the opportunity to hear stories of not only their past, but where they want to be in the future. It is our job to help plan today to best accomplish these goals. This may be starting a savings plan or even how to best spend your life’s savings. The focus is on you and how we can help check the boxes for retirement funding, 401k rollovers, and even beneficiary planning to ensure that assets are transferred to the ones that matter the most to you.

Get in touch about the success of your financial future.
